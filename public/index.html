Telling the brand story is an art!

An art that BizScribe has mastered.

This tiny upstart venture has the vision to offer a new perspective into brand building, through compelling stories generated incorporating feature journalism style, to showcase the many facets of the brand – its personality, the thought leadership, the philosophy and the social and environmental value it generates. An insightful story with an emotional appeal is all what it takes to spark a conversation in the industry and the world around you about your brand.

OUR MISSION

We strive to maintain integrity in crafting the most accurate and authentic brand story for your business.

OUR SERVICES

Social Media Story-telling

Scientific research has indicated how human brain responds to the descriptive power of a story. We create feature style content for social media campaigns to build that emotional connection with the consumer.

CSR and Sustainability Content Creation

Your business has a strong social commitment. But are you communicating ENOUGH? Writing compelling features OR merely reporting? We can help you showcase the brand’s personality through engaging stories generated from humanitarian and sustainability initiatives of your business.

Consultation & Development of Social Media Content Strategy

Helping brands identify unique story ideas to be used in the content strategy for brand building/brand communications.

LinkedIn Influencer AVs

Pair your marketing strategy with the LinkedIn version of blogs we create in the form of AVs to reach the right audience, generate engagement and drive traffic to your social media page.
